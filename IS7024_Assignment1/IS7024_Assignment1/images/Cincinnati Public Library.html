Steps to become member

  1. Bring your photo identity card eg. Driver's license, State ID, etc.
  2. Bring your proof of address showing that you reside in Cincinnati.
  3. Fil out the application form and submit it to libary.
  4. Collect your libary card after 2 business day once submitted the application.
